[[South Korea]]
Policy against Nort Korea. 
[[Liberalism]]

In contrast to french strategic autonomy, that should be achived by increasing its own national capacity (mostly but not limited to nuclear), Korean try to change behavior of their northen neighbor by cooperation, trust building and economical ties.

Instead of regime change or political isolation, Seul tried to create a dialog and provide a humanitarian aid. 

It took inspiration from Willy's Brandt Ostpolitik to the Eastern Block, especialy Easter Germany
'Wandel durch Annaherung'

Was it successful policy? The main goal of preventing war on a pennisula was achived. However after the sunset of the sunshine policy (2008y. and success of a nuclear program made by nort Korea), we still do not have any real conflict betwen states.
The regime did not happend to be any more liberal. 

The Sunshine policy did not changed the strategical position of South Korea in the long term. It did not prevented north Korea from building nuclear weapons and did not made both states more co-dependant.
