"Strike Force"
[[France]]

Independend nuclear force (nuclear deterrence).
Rejection of the U.S. Nuclear umbrella within NATO
It is more of a policy ([[Gaulism]] policy) than the doctrine, can be classified as a part of [[Strategic Autonomy]] doctrine within Realist and Detterence theory.

Main Sources: [[The Balance of Terror]] - Pierre Gallois
'The Great power must posses an independent ability to inflict unacceptable damage on any aggressor, regardless of alliances.'

'Detterence of the strong, by the weak'
The weaker state does not need parity it only needs the capacity to impose unacceptable costs.

'ultimate avertissement' - final warning -> small nuclear attack that would signal determination and force the enemy to stop.

The detterence should work against any opponent, not only USSR but also, for example US.
Nuclear weapon prevents coercive diplomacy, blackmailing, and intimidation.
